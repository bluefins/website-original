Annual Masters Awards

Basingstoke Bluefins Masters hold an annual awards evening to celebrate the Clubs successes and individual performances throughout the year.

The awards up for grabs are:

  • Male Merit Award
  • Female Merit Award
  • Team Spirit Award
  • Outstanding Achievement Award
  • Open Water Award
  • Most Improved Male Award
  • Most Improved Female Award
  • Swim of the Year
  • Male Masters Swimmer of the Year
  • Female Masters Swimmer of the Year
  • Outstanding Performance Award

For the first time, 2014 awards will be voted by BBF club members. In December the final short list chosen by the coach and club captians will be published.

A year is a long time and for some of us our memeory is not as good as it used to be, if you know of a swimmer that deserves to be recognised, please submit their details below and fingers crossed they may be successful in reaching the final short list. Nominations can be completed by friends and family too.

Good luck to all swimmers, have a successful year!
