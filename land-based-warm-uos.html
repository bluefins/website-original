Land Based Warm Ups

From a scientific viewpoint we know that a land based warm up prior to training:

  • Increases body temperature
     
  • Increases heart rate
     
  • Increases blood pressure
     
  • Increases energy producing enzyme activity
     
  • Increases range of joint movement

 

Why Warm up:

  • It assists in preventing injury
     
  • It increases the bodies’ range of movement – allowing relaxed and efficient swimming technique
     
  • Focuses the mind on the task ahead e.g. the training session.

 

Warm up exercises:

  • Light Jogging on the Spot
     
  • Stride Jumps
     
  • Skipping - Light easy skipping, with some vigorous skipping thrown in at intervals
     
  • Arm & Leg Swings – with controlled movements!
     
  • A light general stretching routine working large muscles groups

 

Remember: Your Coach will always be more than happy to work with you to develop a routine which works for you – just ask them, and then make sure you do it!
